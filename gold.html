3.EXCHANGE TRADED FUND(ETF's)

AN ETF IS AN EXCHANGE TRADED FUND, WHICH UNLIKE REGULAR MUTUAL FUNDS TRADES LIKE A COMMON STOCK ON A STOCK EXCHANGE
