Real estate investing can involve purchasing rental properties, REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts), or crowdfunding platforms that allow you to invest in real estate projects.

5.CRYPTO CURRENCIES

Cryptocurrencies are digital assets that use cryptography to secure transactions and control the creation of new units. They are highly volatile and carry significant risk.
